Hack-Nation · Challenge 1 · The Negotiator

Haggl.

An AI advocate that reads a hospital bill, finds the errors and the leverage, then calls the billing office on a real phone line and negotiates the balance down. In the flagship case a $4,287 ER bill settled at $1,650, and every number the agent spoke was computed by code and gated through a tool.
