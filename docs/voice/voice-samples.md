## Voice notes (for the rewriter, not rules to recite)

Greetings are warm and personal, gratitude comes first and is specific, asks are direct but low-pressure with an easy out, dashes are single hyphens, exclamation points are real and sparing, sign-offs are simple, sentences are plain. Nothing performative, nothing that flexes. The give (repo, finding, method) gets mentioned the way Hamza would mention it in person: casually, once, with a link, not as a headline about himself.

One adjustment on top of the natural style (per FINAL-KICKOFF task 2): the old messages sometimes ask to "connect and hear more" without giving anything. New messages always carry one concrete give (the repo, a finding, a method), delivered casually.
